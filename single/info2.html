Forest Hiking

• Adventure Overview:

Embark on a journey through lush green trails, surrounded by towering trees and the soothing sounds of nature. Forest hiking offers breathtaking views, encounters with diverse wildlife, and a refreshing escape from the bustling city. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hiker, this adventure is perfect for reconnecting with nature and enjoying the tranquility of the forest.

• Activity Details:

  • Morning departure and guided trek
  • Scenic trails with various difficulty levels
  • Wildlife spotting and nature photography
  • Picnic lunch and relaxation by a stream

• Difficulty Level and Requirements:

Difficulty: Moderate | Suitable for ages 10 and above.

• Equipment and Gear:

Provided: Guide, first-aid kit, trail maps.

Bring: Hiking boots, backpack, water bottle, energy snacks.

• Safety Measures:

Trained guides, emergency support, safe trails, and designated rest stops.

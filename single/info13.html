Paragliding

• Adventure Overview:

Soar through the skies like a bird and enjoy breathtaking aerial views while experiencing the ultimate adrenaline rush.

• Activity Details:

  • Safety briefing and gear check
  • Takeoff from a scenic hilltop
  • Guided flight with an instructor
  • Landing and debriefing

• Difficulty Level and Requirements:

Difficulty: Moderate | No prior experience required, just a thrill for heights.

• Equipment and Gear:

Provided: Paraglider, harness, helmet.

Bring: Comfortable clothing and sturdy shoes.

• Safety Measures:

Trained pilots, emergency safety systems, and weather checks.
